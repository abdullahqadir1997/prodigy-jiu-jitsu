The Art

What Is Muay Thai?

Muay Thai — the Art of Eight Limbs — is Thailand's national combat sport and one of the most effective striking systems on earth. Where most striking arts use two weapons (hands and feet), Muay Thai utilizes eight: fists, elbows, knees, and kicks.

This is what many people search for when they look for kickboxing classes — and Muay Thai delivers it at a higher, more complete level. Whether your goal is elite fitness, real self-defense capability, or stepping into MMA or competitive Muay Thai, this is where you start.

No prior experience required. Every class is open to all levels.

MMA & Self-Defense

The Most Effective Stand-Up Art in MMA

Look at any elite MMA fighter's striking game and you'll find Muay Thai at the core. The ability to strike with all eight limbs — and dominate the clinch with elbows and knees — makes Muay Thai unmatched for cage fighting.

For self-defense, it's equally powerful. Muay Thai's emphasis on range management, powerful kicks, and clinch control makes it immediately practical in real-world situations.
